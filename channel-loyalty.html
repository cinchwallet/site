Channel Loyalty

Your Channel Partners are the key to increase your sales revenues

A channel partner loyalty program offers channel partners something that motivates, rouses, or encourages specific behavior to the vendor’s brand. Loyalty programs typically operate over longer periods or indefinitely and reward repeat sales of the vendor’s products. A well executed channel incentive program creates a virtuous circle in which both channel partner and vendor are motivated to collaborate to attain improved performance and achieve pre-defined goals.

CinchWallet have extensive experience creating and running end-to-end Channel Partners Loyalty, Recognition and Incentive Programs that drive your indirect sales and strengthen relationships with the partners, dealers, distributors and resellers.

From the vendors perspective, good candidates for channel partners loyalty programs are vendors who:

Want to reward partner loyalty

Suffer from poor partner loyalty

Want to reward partner investment and commitment

Enjoy a more mature or intimate relationship with their partners

CinchWallet is your partner with the expertise, tools and services to create a complete, end-to-end loyalty program and to manage every detail of your loyalty and incentive programs, including program design, technology platform, participant communications, program management, reward management, operations management and performance management.

When your footprint spans across various retail formats including exclusive stores, multi-brand stores, and Large Format Retail (LFR) outlets, fostering relationships with partners becomes critical to have a big share of their mind-space. A continuous engagement with channel partners through an incentive program is therefore imperative to ensure that your channel sales team is focusing adequately on selling your products. Such a program not only helps achieving overall sales objectives, but also enables defining individual targets for each channel partner.

Channel Loyalty Program service elements

  • Solution & scheme design aligned with your budgets and target markets
  • IT system implementation – partner enrollment and database management
  • Program branding and multi-channel marketing communication
  • Channel partner engagement through events and road shows
  • Reward catalogue development, sourcing and fulfillment
  • Claim verification & stock reconciliation through web based software module
  • Training, accreditation and certification
  • Website design, development and maintenance
