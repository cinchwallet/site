Gift Cards

Gift cards are the most popular gift. Customers love them. So do businesses. Here’s why: They look great in the store, branded with your company logo. They take up little room, leaving your precious retail real estate free for product. Most gift cards aren’t redeemed right away. This allows you to bank the cash and collect interest or use it for other operations. Many gift cards are never completely used, leaving a balance for you to claim as profit.

Gift cards can be branded with your company name or logo on a pre-designed card suitable for your industry. You can also create a fully-customized gift card design for your business. Customers can load cards with any amount ranging from Rs. 1 to a maximum of Rs.10000.

Benefits of Gift Card

Help increase sales and gain new customers

Extend your brand with eye-catching card designs

Reduce fraud – cards hold no value until they are activated

Easy setup and processing through your existing Moneris terminal

Flexibility – customers decide the amount they wish to load or reload

Additional sales channel online – you can sell gift cards online through your very own branded Gift Card Web Store

Run promotions – Market your business using promotional gift cards with expiry rules

In addition to increased customer spending, gift cards are a valuable tool for many reasons:

Gift cards are reloadable (sometimes) and can help you repeat the process and increase profit

Gift card customers are more likely to purchase items at full price, allowing you to keep healthy profit margins

Less merchandise returns. Gift cards allow customers to purchase what they want, greatly decreasing the return of merchandise and allowing your employees to be more productive

Top seven reasons Gift cards are more effective than Cash

TROPHY VALUE : Gift Card purchases are more memorable – they create a lasting reminder of achievement
VIRAL VALUE : Recipients tell each other how they redeemed their gift
MORE DISCRETE : It’s polite to discuss receiving a gift card
TRUE GIFT PERCEPTION : Gift Cards are not viewed as part of compensation
GUILT FREE SPENDING : Recipients don’t feel guilty treating themselves
PLEASING LOVED ONES : There is a strong motivation to earn an award for one’s significant other or children
LONG TERM POSITIVE FEELINGS TOWARD ORGANIZATION : Gift Cards reinforce positive associations with sponsoring organizations
