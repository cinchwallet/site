About Us

About Us

ClichWallet is a Loyalty and Gift card technology partner focused to developing loyalty & gift/prepaid card products and acts as end to end technology services provider. Specialised in Gift and Loyalty solution with established processing framework, CinchWallet takes care of end-to-end solution for merchant/retailers to design & establish Loyalty/Gift system for them. Providing enriched UI for management of the cards/customer, customising their card programs by their own.

We have 30 years of combined industry experience, highly Qualified, Committed, Nimble and Customer/Consumer focused team.

Who we are

CinchWallet is a loyalty, gift card, marketing, and consulting company. Our team has a combined 30+ years of experience with loyalty in areas including Retail (offline and online), Payments, Financial Services and Loyalty Coalitions.

What we do

In simple words, we help business get loyalty right.
We do this by creating great new loyalty programs, operating and optimizing existing programs, and delivering personalized targeted marketing services which make better use of customer data.

At CinchWallet, we apply insights, innovations, and best practices from across the globe to provide the right solutions for our clients. Clients get loyalty solutions which are tied to their specific strategies, and that produce directly measurable results

How can we help

We partner with businesses to help plan and execute loyalty and marketing strategy by leveraging loyalty tools and customer data to drive sales growth, market share, customer acquisition and retention.

We are experts in building and managing loyalty programs, and increasing the effectiveness of marketing programs across multiple industries.

CinchWallet is an experienced, market-proven service provided of complete gift, loyalty and stored-value card systems to the retail, restaurant and service industries.

Our range of products and services allow small, single-location merchants and franchise companies with hundreds of locations to implement a variety of highly-profitable card programs.

CinchWallet is focused on providing results-getting products, systems and services at the lowest possible cost without cutting corners on quality.

We believe that paying a little less for an inferior product is ultimately not in the best interest of our customers. Gift cards are a product just like anything else in your inventory. How your gift cards and packaging looks and feels can affect sales by 100% or more.
