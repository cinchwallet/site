Loyalty Commerce

Loyalty program is planned/structured marketing effort to reward the customer and encourage them buying more and more. In modern day, loyalty programs are spread across various categories like fuel, hotels, book stores, credit cards, airlines. Member of such program earn points against their shopping done with specific retailers stores and further these earned points can be redeemed for gift, shopping and services.

A successful Customer Loyalty Strategy delivers on the following promise: -> Higher customer retention
-> Higher customer engagement
-> Increased adoption
-> Increased customer lifetime value


= Higher overall revenue for Brands 

Give customers an incentive to shop with you. Create promotions that assign loyalty points: each time cardholders shop with you they’ll receive points towards special offers or discounts. Later on points can be redeems against some purchase to enhance customer experience and show them value of their money/expenses.

How it works

-> Create system rules ($ per points etc.)

-> Register customer detail in database and give them loyalty card

-> Customers got awarded with point each time they spend.

-> User online system for data analysis and create market campaigns.

-> Customer return to redeem their points and keep shopping.

Loyalty Programs Options

We offer various styles of loyalty programs that will compliment your customer retention strategy.

Point Programs: The points program will allow to award points for money spent. A versatile program, allowing you to award point-to-rupee ratio. You can configure custom reward levels to be reached, and also create special promotions to give you complete control over your program.

Buy X Get One Free: Reward customers based on multiple product purchases.

Action-Based Program: Reward customers based on interactions or events instead of amount spent. Most commonly used as a referral solution.

Coalition Program: A specialty program that incorporates multiple merchants under one loyalty program.

Loyalty Rewards

Acknowledge new buyers with a reward for first time purchase Example can be quoted here for 8 service vs 10 (2 are given as rewards)

Rewards must be instantly available or if the customer chooses, accrued toward future purchases.

Rewards must be meaningful - if you are selling online, redemption of rewards should be available online.

Rewards can be low cost, but must have high value to consumers to be effective.

Communicate future rewards expectations - let them know why they should buy from you going forward .
